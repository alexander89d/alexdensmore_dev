Accessibility Statement

When producing and updating this website, my goal is to make it accessible to all. Having a visual impairment myself, accessibility is very important to me. I have been teaching myself various aspects of digital accessibility while creating this website. For example, I have taught myself about HTML5 semantic tags for making the purpose of various sections clearer to screen reader users, making use of such tags as <nav> and <main>. In addition, I have researched best practices such as making menus accessible . Since I am using this website in part to teach myself how to make websites accessible, please feel free to contact me with any feedback regarding any website components that are not currently accessible to you.
